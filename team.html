Our Team

Sonore  has developed a reputation in the community for its excellent instruction. Our coaches have studied at some of the finest music schools and conservatories in the world. But more importantly, they sincerely care about their students and are committed to providing learning experiences that fosters a life-long love of music.

Founder, CEO
