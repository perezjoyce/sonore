Who We Are

Sonore  

... is a music center that aims to cater to students of all ages and musical backgrounds. It means "resonant" and "with rich tone" in French-- words that aptly describe our school's culture. Our ultimate goal is to promote joy and fun of music making so students can enrich themselves and others in the process.

Classes

Our programs are designed with your needs in mind. No matter where you are in terms of experience and background, you can definitely achieve your musical aspirations with our help.

VOICE

Acquire great vocal techniques with our voice classes.

STRINGED

Learn to play instruments like guitar, bass, violin and cielo.

PIANO

Our piano classes provides in-depth knowledge and training for aspiring pianists.

Drums

Our lessons will help you unlock your full potential on the drums.

BRASS

We offer extensive trumpet learning program at our music school.

TRADITIONAL
Learn to play any traditional Filipino and asian instruments of your choice.
