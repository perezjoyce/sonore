Frequently Asked Questions FAQs

  • What programs do you offer?

    We offer lessons in different instruments, including voice, guitar, piano, drums, saxophone and violin.

  • What makes you different from other music schools?

    Our coaches are some of the most highly-regarded musicians in the country. Our programs are rich, and diverse, able to cater to every musical need, from the youngest beginner to the most fastidious professional-in-waiting. But most of all, we promote the joy and fun of music making! It’s why we do it ourselves, after all.

  • What are the schedules of your classes?

    Group classes have specific schedules. Please refer to the schedules posted on the page for your preferred class: voice, guitar, piano, drums, saxophone or violin.

    On the other hand, the schedule for 1-on-1 coaching can be adjusted depending on the availability of both the student and the music coach. In interested, pease contact us regarding this.

  • How much are your classes?

    We offer a fixed rate of only PHP 6,000 per month for small group classes, regardless of the instrument you choose. At the moment, price varies on a case by case basis for 1-on-1 coaching. If interested, please contact us regarding this.

  • What should I do if I don't already have an instrument?

    That’s not a problem at all! If you sign up for any string instrument, we have a selection of high quality rental instruments through our partnership with JB Music. Your teacher can help size you at your first lesson to make sure you get the right instrument that suits your needs. All students who enroll with us also receive a Student Discount Card for special discounts at JB's.

  • Am I too young/old for this?

    We accept students from all ages! If there is a will and ability to work with a coach for at least two hours a week, there is no such thing as “too young" or "too old.”

  • When can I start?

    You can start anytime, as long as slots are available. Please contact us regarding this or visit the pages of your preferred class for updates: voice, guitar, piano, drums, saxophone or violin.
